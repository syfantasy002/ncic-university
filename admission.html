Welcome to New Canaan International College

Thank you for your interest in New Canaan International College. We offer transformative educational experiences for students, combining theoretical knowledge with practical skills to address today's global challenges.

We focus on developing critical thinking, innovation, and ethical leadership to help students succeed in their future careers. At NCIC, you will join a diverse learning community, growing alongside outstanding students and faculty from around the world.

If you are ready to embark on a life-changing academic journey, we invite you to apply to join our vibrant university community.

Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Admission Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 (on a 4.0 scale) or equivalent
  • English language proficiency (e.g., TOEFL 80+ or IELTS 6.5+)
  • Two letters of recommendation (at least one from a teacher)
  • Personal statement/motivation letter
  • Extracurricular activities and achievements
  • Standardized test scores (SAT or ACT, optional)
  • $50 application fee (non-refundable)

Graduate Admission Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 3.2 (on a 4.0 scale) or equivalent
  • English language proficiency (e.g., TOEFL 90+ or IELTS 7.0+)
  • Two letters of recommendation (at least one from a professor)
  • Research proposal or statement of purpose
  • Resume/curriculum vitae
  • GRE or GMAT scores (as required by program)
  • $75 application fee (non-refundable)

Application Process

Step 1: Create an Online Account

Visit our online application portal, create a personal account, and begin the application process.

Step 2: Complete the Application Form

Fill out all required information, including personal details, academic background, and chosen program.

Step 3: Upload Required Documents

Upload all necessary documents, including transcripts, letters of recommendation, and language proficiency proof.

Step 4: Pay the Application Fee

Pay the non-refundable application fee using our secure online payment system.

Step 5: Review and Evaluation

Your application will be reviewed by our admissions committee, who will evaluate all aspects of your submission.

Step 6: Admission Decision

You will receive an admission decision via email, typically within 4-6 weeks after your complete application is received.

Frequently Asked Questions

We offer admissions for both Fall and Spring semesters. The application window for Fall semester typically opens in October of the previous year, and for Spring semester in May of the previous year. Please refer to the "Important Dates" section above for specific deadlines.

Applicants whose native language is not English need to provide proof of English language proficiency. We accept TOEFL (minimum 80 for undergraduate, 90 for graduate), IELTS (minimum 6.5 for undergraduate, 7.0 for graduate), or Duolingo English Test (minimum 105 for undergraduate, 115 for graduate). If you have completed at least two years of study at a school where English is the primary language of instruction, you may apply for a language requirement waiver.

Transfer applicants need to provide official transcripts from all previously attended institutions, course descriptions, proof of credits completed, and evidence of good academic standing. Our admissions committee will evaluate which credits can transfer into our programs. Generally, courses with a GPA of at least 3.0 can be considered for transfer credit.

Most scholarships require separate applications. After completing your admission application, you will have access to the scholarship application portal. Some merit-based scholarships automatically consider all eligible applicants without a separate application. We recommend applying for scholarships early, as funds are limited and awarded on a first-come, first-served basis.

Yes, you can select a primary major and an alternate major in your application. This is especially useful for competitive programs. If your application to your primary major is not accepted, we will automatically consider you for your alternate choice without requiring you to reapply.
