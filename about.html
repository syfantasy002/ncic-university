Our Story

New Canaan International College was founded in 2005 with a vision to create a globally recognized institution that combines academic excellence with innovative approaches to learning. What began as a small institution with just 200 students has now evolved into a thriving international university with over 5,000 students from more than 70 countries.

Our journey has been marked by continuous growth, academic achievements, and a commitment to preparing students for the challenges of a rapidly changing world. Through strategic partnerships with leading global institutions and industry leaders, we've created a unique educational ecosystem that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and practical skills development.

Today, NCIC stands as a beacon of educational innovation in Central Asia, offering a diverse range of undergraduate and graduate programs designed to meet the demands of the 21st-century workforce.

Our Mission

To foster academic excellence, promote innovative research, and cultivate a diverse community of leaders committed to addressing global challenges through cross-cultural understanding and sustainable solutions.

We are dedicated to providing a transformative educational experience that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to thrive in a complex, interconnected world and make meaningful contributions to society.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution of higher learning that leads in educational innovation, research excellence, and the development of ethical leaders who drive positive change in their communities and beyond.

We envision a future where NCIC graduates are distinguished by their intellectual curiosity, cultural competence, entrepreneurial mindset, and commitment to creating a more just, peaceful, and sustainable world.

Accreditation & Recognition

New Canaan International College is proud to be accredited by leading national and international educational authorities, ensuring that our programs meet the highest standards of academic quality and relevance.

  • International Association of Universities (IAU) - Full membership since 2010
  • Association of Central Asian Universities (ACAU) - Founding member
  • Kyrgyz National Accreditation Council - Institutional and program accreditation
  • U.S. Regional Accreditation Partnership - Affiliated status
  • European Quality Improvement System (EQUIS) - Business School accreditation

Our accreditations ensure that degrees from NCIC are recognized worldwide, providing our graduates with enhanced opportunities for further education and employment globally.
