Vibrant Campus Experience

At New Canaan International College, we believe that a comprehensive educational experience extends beyond the classroom. We provide a dynamic campus environment where you can explore interests, make lifelong friends, participate in meaningful activities, and broaden your horizons.

From student clubs and cultural events to sports competitions and volunteer opportunities, our campus life is filled with enriching experiences and growth opportunities. Whether you're an art enthusiast, sports champion, future leader, or cultural explorer, NCIC offers platforms for self-realization.

Join our community to discover all aspects of campus life and create a unique and memorable college experience.

Student Accommodation

Comfortable Modern Living

We provide safe, comfortable, modern student dormitories to help you enjoy convenient campus living. All residence halls are equipped with high-speed WiFi, laundry facilities, study areas, and recreational spaces.

Our accommodation options include:

  • Standard Double Rooms
  • Single Suites
  • Apartment-Style Residences
  • Themed Living-Learning Communities

All dormitories have professional residence staff and security personnel to ensure your safety and comfort. First-year students are guaranteed on-campus housing, while upperclassmen can choose between off-campus apartments or continued on-campus living.

Modern Amenities

High-speed WiFi, smart access systems, modern furniture, air conditioning, and central heating provide a comfortable living environment.

Security Measures

24/7 security services, surveillance systems, emergency response protocols, and electronic access control ensure student safety is our top priority.

Community Support

Professional residence staff, resident advisors, and peer mentor programs help you adapt to campus life and address daily concerns.

Campus Dining

Diverse Dining Options

Our campus offers diverse, healthy, and delicious dining options to meet the needs of students from different cultural backgrounds and dietary preferences. From international cuisine to local specialties, from quick meals to fine dining, you'll find satisfying dining experiences at NCIC.

We value food quality, nutrition, and sustainability while striving to accommodate special dietary needs including vegetarian, halal, and gluten-free options.

Student Activities and Clubs

Diverse Extracurricular Activities

At NCIC, we encourage students to expand their horizons, develop skills, and build social networks through participation in diverse extracurricular activities. We offer over 100 student clubs and organizations covering academics, culture, arts, sports, volunteer service, and leadership development.

Whether you wish to explore academic interests, showcase artistic talents, engage in sports competitions, or serve the community, you'll find suitable organizations and activities here.

Our student activities center organizes various events throughout the year, from themed parties and cultural celebrations to lecture series and campus competitions, providing you with a rich campus experience.

Student Support Services

Comprehensive Health & Counseling Services

We care about students' physical and mental health, providing comprehensive health and counseling services to ensure you receive necessary support during your studies.

Our campus medical center offers basic medical services, health consultations, and emergency care. The counseling center consists of professional counselors providing individual counseling, group counseling, and crisis intervention services.

All services are strictly confidential, respecting student privacy. We are committed to creating a caring, inclusive, and supportive campus environment.

Campus Culture

New Canaan International College has a rich and diverse campus culture, integrating traditions, celebrations, and festivals from different countries and regions. We respect diversity and encourage students to share their cultural backgrounds while exploring other cultures.

An annual celebration showcasing various cultures, traditional costumes, cuisines, and artistic performances, one of the most popular events on campus.
